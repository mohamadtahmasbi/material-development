Passage

A Visit to the Doctor

Maria has not been feeling well for the past week. She has had a persistent cough and a high fever. Finally, she decided to visit the hospital. At the hospital, she was greeted by a friendly nurse who took her temperature and checked her blood pressure. The nurse asked Maria if she had experienced any other symptoms. Maria replied, "Yes, I have also had a sore throat and headaches." The nurse then took Maria to see the physician. The physician has been working at the hospital for over ten years and has a lot of experience with similar cases. After examining Maria, the physician said, "It seems like you have an infection. I will prescribe you some antibiotics." Maria was relieved to hear that she would be getting treatment. The physician explained, "You need to take these antibiotics for seven days. Make sure you follow the dosage instructions carefully." Before leaving, Maria visited the pharmacy to collect her medication. The pharmacist there asked, "Have you taken antibiotics before?" Maria nodded and said, "Yes, I have taken antibiotics once for an ear infection." The pharmacist reminded her of the importance of completing the entire course of medication. Maria thanked the pharmacist and left the hospital feeling hopeful that she would recover soon.
